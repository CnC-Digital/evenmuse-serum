Frequently Asked Questions

How long before I see results? ▼
Most users notice improvement in 2–4 weeks with consistent AM/PM use + sunscreen. Full results around 30 days. Individual results may vary.
Is it safe for sensitive skin? ▼
Yes. Alpha arbutin is one of the gentlest brightening actives available. No peeling, no stinging. But always patch test first. Apply a small amount on your inner arm for 24 hours before full use.
How is this different from a niacinamide serum? ▼
Niacinamide evens tone generally. Alpha arbutin targets the SOURCE of dark spots. It blocks melanin production at the root. Our formula actually has BOTH (10% Niacinamide + 2% Alpha Arbutin) to deliver better results.
Is it safe for pregnant or breastfeeding women? ▼
Consult your OB first. Alpha arbutin is generally considered safe topically, but we always recommend professional advice for pregnant and breastfeeding moms.
